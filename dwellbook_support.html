Getting started

Using DwellBook

DwellBook stores everything on your iPhone and syncs privately through your own iCloud account. There are no accounts to create, no passwords to remember, and no data ever stored on our servers.

To get started, simply download the app, tap "Add your first task", and begin building your home's record. We recommend starting with the pre-loaded task library — it covers the most common home maintenance jobs with suggested intervals already set.

The one-time purchase unlocks unlimited tasks, widgets, and partner sharing. To restore a purchase on a new device, tap Settings → Restore Purchase inside the app while signed into the same Apple ID you used originally.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: DwellBook does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. Your home's records live on your device and in your own iCloud account. We never see them.

Who we are

DwellBook is an iOS application for home maintenance tracking. References to "we," "us," or "DwellBook" in this policy refer to the developer of the DwellBook application.

What data we collect

We collect no personal data. DwellBook does not require an account, does not have a login, and does not transmit any user-generated content to our servers — because we have no servers.

All data you enter into DwellBook — tasks, contractors, paint colours, photos, warranty records, measurements — is stored exclusively:

  • On your iPhone, iPad, or Mac local storage
  • In your personal iCloud account, if iCloud sync is enabled

We have no access to this data. It is governed entirely by Apple's iCloud terms of service and privacy policy.

Analytics and crash reporting

DwellBook may use Apple's optional, anonymised crash reporting through Xcode's crash log system. This data is collected by Apple, not by us, and contains no personally identifiable information. It is used solely to identify and fix bugs. You can opt out of this by turning off "Share with App Developers" in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

Purchases

DwellBook's one-time purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never receive, store, or process your payment information. Apple's purchase privacy policy governs all transaction data.

Third-party services

DwellBook does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We use no SDKs that collect data. There are no ads in DwellBook and there never will be.

Children's privacy

DwellBook is designed for homeowners and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Data retention

Because we collect no data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. Your data exists only in your iCloud account and on your devices. You can delete it at any time by deleting the app or clearing your iCloud storage.

Your rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Since your data lives entirely in your own iCloud account, you exercise these rights directly through Apple's iCloud controls — not through us.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and note the change in the next DwellBook app update. We will never introduce data collection practices that contradict this policy without clear prior notice.
